Meat Jelly

Description

Meat jelly is delicacy that graces the earth. This is made using meat to create a geletin, and spices to make this taste good.

Ingredients

  • Beef Shank 1.5kg
  • Pepper peas 25g
  • NaCl 10g
  • Onions 100g
  • Garlic 40g

Steps

  1. Rinse meat and put it in a pot. Pour 3 litres of cold water in the pot and boil on low heat for 6 hours
  2. Add vegetables in pot after 5 hours of boiling meat on low.
  3. After 6 hours remove meat from pot, and discard vegetables.
  4. Pull meat off of bones, and place into bowls
  5. Sieve the meat stock. Then pour into bowls and place in fridge for at least 2 hours.
