Broodwhich

Description

This sandwhich is irrestible. It has on hell of a taste, and should be made with caution.

Ingredients

  • Bread Flour 510g
  • Instant yeast 10g
  • Sugar 30g
  • Whole milk 1 cup
  • Egg 1
  • Oil 3Tbsp
  • Salt 2Tsp
  • Red Coloring 3 big squirts
  • Turkey Breast
  • Kosher Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Salt and pepper turkey on a wire rack
  2. Place in fridge overnight then roast at 300 degrees for 45 minutes
  3. Combine all bread ingredients in mixing bowl except for red coloring
  4. While dough is mixing add red coloring until dough is a light red
  5. Knead dough until it does not stick to sides and has smooth texture
  6. Let the dough rest for an hour
  7. Cut 1 small portion of dough and roll rest into a 16x12 rectnagle and twist into a roll
  8. Shape small portions into tail and horn
  9. Let dough rest for 45 minutes at room temperature
  10. Bake at 350 for 18 minutes
  11. Let bread cool completley after taking out of the oven
  12. With the bread and meat completed forge the sandwhich with toppings of your choosing
